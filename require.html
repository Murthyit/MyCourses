What is Requirements Elicitation?

Requirements elicitation is the process of gathering, discovering, and understanding stakeholder needs for a software system. It helps analysts identify what users expect from the system.

Elicitation Process

Identify Stakeholders
Gather Requirements
Analyze Needs
Document Requirements
Validate Requirements

Elicitation Techniques

Interview

One-to-one discussion.

Questionnaire

Collect feedback from many users.

Observation

Watch users perform tasks.

Workshop

Collaborative requirement gathering.

Brainstorming

Generate innovative ideas.

Prototyping

Create early system models.
